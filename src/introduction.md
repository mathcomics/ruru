# Introduction

*Ruru* is a story about — well, you have to read it to find out.

The first two volumes were written in 2016, during an online writing course with Otis Haschemyer at Stanford University. Then the manuscript sat in my drive for ten years. When I finally returned to it, I handed my plotline for the third chapter to Claude to complete. A few revisions later, here we are.
